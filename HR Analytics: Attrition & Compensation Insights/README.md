# Background

In the field of Human Resource Management, analyzing employee-related data helps uncover patterns and drivers behind employee attrition, compensation fairness, job satisfaction, and career development.

Understanding these factors is critical for:

- Optimizing recruitment and retention strategies  
- Improving performance evaluation systems  
- Designing fair and competitive compensation structures  
- Enhancing employee engagement and long-term organizational sustainability  

By leveraging multi-dimensional workforce data, organizations can implement data-driven HR policies that improve productivity, employee loyalty, and overall performance.

# Problem Statements

## 1. Employee Attrition Analysis

- Identify key factors associated with attrition (e.g., satisfaction, salary, commute distance).  
- Analyze attrition patterns across age groups, marital status, and tenure.  

---

## 2. Compensation Fairness Analysis

- Compare salary differences across Gender, Education, and EducationField.  
- Evaluate the relationship between JobLevel / JobRole and income metrics (MonthlyIncome, HourlyRate, DailyRate).  

---

## 3. Job Satisfaction & Performance Analysis

- Assess relationships between JobSatisfaction, EnvironmentSatisfaction, RelationshipSatisfaction, and PerformanceRating.  
- Analyze WorkLifeBalance in relation to JobInvolvement and YearsAtCompany.  

---

## 4. Career Development & Promotion Path Analysis

- Examine the relationship between YearsSinceLastPromotion, JobLevel, and PerformanceRating.  
- Analyze how YearsInCurrentRole impacts job involvement and promotion opportunities.  

---

## 5. Training & Development Needs Assessment

- Evaluate the relationship between TrainingTimesLastYear and PerformanceRating.  
- Analyze TotalWorkingYears in relation to training demand.  

---

## 6. Benefits & Incentive Impact Analysis

- Explore the effect of StockOptionLevel on employee retention.  
- Assess the relationship between PercentSalaryHike and satisfaction / performance.  

---

## 7. Workforce Planning & Predictive Modeling

- Identify predictive factors influencing employee retention.  
- Build models to estimate attrition risk and potential promotion pathways.  

---
